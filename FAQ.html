This is probably not a great trip for kids. Because our target audience are experienced rafters who have extensive backgrounds with Class I through III rapids and who are looking to expand their skills and confidence in taking on Class IV through VI rapids. We work with individuals who are looking to partner up with existing groups as well as previously formed rafting crews who are looking to level up together as a crew. Our initial trips begin right at Class IV rapids and so we must emphasize that this is not a service for beginners or for groups looking for a calm float down the river! Experience navigating Class III rapids is a prerequisite for our floats. In short it is more so a question of experience then age
